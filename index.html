貪行性（Rāga） ⌄

AN 3.68 · SA 1049

“由於貪欲行，心隨貪轉，則於法不正受。”

"Driven by greed, the mind bends toward desire and misreceives the Dharma."

“若心著於欲，則不知如實。”

"A mind clinging to sensual desire does not see things as they truly are."

瞋行性（Dosa） ⌄

AN 3.68 · SA 1049

“由於瞋恚行，心隨瞋轉，則於法不正受。”

"When driven by aversion, the mind becomes harsh and cannot receive the Dharma properly."

“心若被恚覆，則不能了知。”

"A mind veiled by hatred cannot discern clearly."

癡行性（Moha） ⌄

AN 3.68 · SA 1049

“由於愚癡行，心隨癡轉，則於法不正受。”

"When driven by delusion, the mind becomes confused and fails to grasp the Dharma."

“愚癡覆心者，不見真實。”

"One whose mind is clouded by delusion cannot see truth."

信根（Saddhindriya） ⌄

SN 48 · SA 791

“信者，能淨其心。”

"Faith purifies the mind."

“如水清澈，信令心明淨。”

"Just as clear water reflects forms, faith makes the mind bright and clean."

慧根（Paññindriya） ⌄

AN 1.49 · SA 296

“慧者，觀察諸法因緣所生。”

"One endowed with wisdom observes that all phenomena arise from causes and conditions."

“慧能破闇。”

"Wisdom dispels darkness."

信行人（Saddhānusārī） ⌄

AN 6.46 · EA 27.7 · Taishō T2

“未能以慧見，而能隨信受者，是名信行。”

"One who does not yet see with wisdom, but follows through trust, is called a follower-by-faith."

“以信為先導，心柔順、易攝受。”

"Led by faith, the heart is gentle and receptive."

法行人（Dhammānusārī） ⌄

AN 6.46 · EA 27.7 · Taishō T2

“能以慧見，如實知法者，是名法行。”

"One who sees the Dhamma with wisdom, knowing it as it truly is, is called a follower-by-Dhamma."

“以慧為先導，心明利、善觀察。”

"Led by wisdom, the mind becomes sharp and discerning."
